Click & Push - Atlas
×
Click & Push is a company I've been involved with for 8 months as an intern, and provides the Atlas: a mobile app that provides crowdfunded accessibility information to users with accessibility limitations as well as companies and institutions who are interested in improving the accessibility of their spaces.

It is built with React Native, and uses Django, Docker, Sqlite, and Nginx on the on the backend.

Elixr - Marketplace
×
Elixr is a platform that aims to curate extremely high quality applications that are backed by real, rigorous science. The marketplace, built with ASP.NET Core Razor Pages, is a web app that provides a way for users to purchase applications that have passed the Elixr review process, or submit their own apps for review. It also aims provides a means for potential app publishers and credentialed app reviewers to interact with each other.

The app is still undergoing development and is not live - currently it supports user authentication, the submission of apps, a simple cart system, and allows apps to undergo the review process.


(All intellectual property is owned by Elixr - https://www.elixrsim.com/)
Medicine Hat Food Bank - Volunteer HR Manager
×
A project that was spawned from a collaboration between my hometown's college and local food bank. It's comprised of an ASP.NET Core Razor Pages web app, a Xamarin forms mobile app and lots of love. It served as a way to

  • provide a means of registration to prospective volunteers
  • keep track of volunteer information,
  • schedule volunteer shifts,
  • keep track of time worked by volunteers.

It was built with ASP.NET Core, Blazor, MariaDB, and IdentityServer4.
